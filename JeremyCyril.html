World Leaders 2026 — Character Taxonomy

Quadrants are explicit character types: Evil, Spineless, Inept, and Principled. Click a point for a photo and full rationale.

Methodology

Three dimensions: intent (pattern of disregard for human life), capability (demonstrated expertise and competence), and agency (how constrained by forces outside their control).

Four character types:

Evil — deliberate harm.

Spineless — knows better, does less.

Inept — doesn't understand the situation well enough to act on their own interests.

Principled — good intent, high capability, acts on both. Currently empty.

Ineptitude is rare by design. Systems cull it before people reach the top. When you see it, it's usually domain-specific. Evil dominates because the system rewards it.

Leaders can occupy more than one category.

The constraint dimension is encoded as colour saturation and separates won't from can't.
